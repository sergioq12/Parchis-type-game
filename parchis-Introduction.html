Rules and Gameplay:

  1. Every player is going to have a turn.
  2. When a player initiates its turn, the player will throw two dices.
  3. The player can choose between moving the added result of the dices or
    move one of the results of one of the two dices.
  4. If the player moves to a tile where the other player is, the other player
    will move back to the start.
  5. The player that gets to the finish line, wins!
  6. The user has to get the exact number to get into the end tile. If the player selects
    a move that is greater to the end, then the player will not move, and it will lose the turn.
